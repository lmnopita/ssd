# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners of fearful, reactive, anxious, or otherwise sensitive dogs in North Seattle (Maple Leaf, Roosevelt, Wedgwood, Ravenna, Fremont, Wallingford). They are often stressed, guilty, or burned by past walkers who gave up on — or were afraid of — their dog. They arrive skeptical and emotionally raw; the job to be done is "find someone I can trust with my difficult dog." The site must lower their guard, not sell at them.

## Product Purpose

Static marketing site for Seattle Sensitive Dogs, a solo force-free dog walker (Lily) specializing in fearful/reactive dogs. Success = a warm, credible first impression that converts a nervous owner into booking a $35 Mellow Meet-and-Greet (currently via the contact page; later a Stripe payment link).

## Brand Personality

Caregiver archetype: calm, warm, quietly authoritative. Three words: attuned, patient, honest. Taglines: "Trust builds at their pace." / "Calm guidance. Real connection." Voice is first-person ("I" = Lily), radically transparent (openly states what's not yet certified/insured). The visual world is premium Pacific-Northwest editorial — quality stationery, evergreen and hill motifs, generous whitespace, soft organic curves.

## Anti-references

- Startup/SaaS landing-page grammar: hero metrics, purple gradients, glassmorphism, emoji, uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section.
- Stock-photo pet-industry cheer (bright primary colors, cartoon paw prints, bouncy fonts).
- Anything that reads AI-templated or salesy/urgent — urgency is the exact opposite of this brand.

## Design Principles

1. Practice what you preach: the site itself should feel like a calm walk — unhurried pacing, no pressure, one idea per fold.
2. Honesty is the aesthetic: placeholder testimonials are labeled as placeholders; transparency notes stay visible.
3. Type carries the brand until photos exist: Cormorant Garamond display + Inter body (committed brand kit — identity-preservation overrides greenfield font rules).
4. Palette is fixed brand kit: deep forest #2d4139, sage #7b8572, terracotta #bf7151 (large/decorative; darkened #a05a3d–#8a4c32 for interactive/small text), cream #ecdecb tint, warm off-white #f7f2e9 base, mist blue #b4c3c6.
5. Every CTA leads to the low-stakes meet-and-greet; never more than one competing action per section.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Verified contrast pairs only (ink 8.2:1+ on tints; accent small text uses #8a4c32 at 5.0:1+). Semantic HTML5, one h1 per page, skip link, :focus-visible styles, prefers-reduced-motion honored, 360px–1440px+ responsive, no content gated on JS.
